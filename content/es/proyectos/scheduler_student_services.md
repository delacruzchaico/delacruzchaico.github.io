## Hey there! :wave:

Welcome to the **Welcome Team Scheduler** — a **Streamlit** app designed to help students at **Collège LaSalle Montréal** sign up to be part of the Welcome Team, assisting new students at the beginning of each semester. :school: :graduation_cap:

## :rocket: Purpose

Starting a new semester can be overwhelming for newcomers. This app makes it easy to organize helpful student volunteers who are eager to support and guide their peers during orientation week. :handshake: :speech_balloon:

---

## :wrench: Features

- :clipboard: **Student Registration**  
  Students can register quickly through a user-friendly form.

- :calendar: **Availability Selection**  
  Volunteers can choose the days and time blocks they're available to help.

- :bar_chart: **Admin Dashboard**  
  Admins can view, filter, and export volunteer data and schedules.

- :inbox_tray: **Schedule Overview**  
  Visual overview of selected availability for better coordination.

- :envelope: **Confirmation Emails** *(optional)*  
  Automatic email confirmations can be enabled for volunteers.
